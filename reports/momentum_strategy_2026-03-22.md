## Implementation Summary

### Strategy Overview
The MomentumAgent implements a pure momentum-based trading strategy using:
- **RSI (Relative Strength Index)** - Mean reversion signals
- **Rate of Change (ROC)** - Momentum magnitude
- **Trend Analysis** - Linear regression for trend direction
- **Volume Confirmation** - High volume as signal confirmation

### Code Location
- `src/agents/MomentumAgent.ts` - Main implementation
- `src/agents/index.ts` - Added to agent factory as "Zeta-Momentum"

- RSI Period: 14

- Position Size: 8% of available capital
- Stop Loss: 5% (buy) / 5% (sell)
- Take Profit: 10% (buy) / 10% (sell)
